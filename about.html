FLUID SEALS INDIA® AN ISO 9001 : 2015 CERTIFIED COMPANY

incorporated an in the year 2012, is India's leading manufacturer of Mechanical Seals & all types of Pump Spares. Our company pioneers in Designing, Manufacturing and Distribution of precision engineered Mechanical Seals and Fluid Sealing Solutions. We have set bench marks on performance, cost and service in our line of products and services offered by us. Our philosophy while attending a seal requirement has been to develop and supply spares for seals giving good performance or develop alternative design to enhance seal performance.

FLUID SEALS INDIA® suit standard pumps like, BHEL-WEIR, EBARA, Investa, Indian Dairy, Flowserve, Kishore, Khimline Sulzer, Kirloskar Brothers, Mather Platt, Microfinish, Raje Dia, Roto, Sam Turbo, Stork Johnson, Sulzer, Tushaco & Special Seals have been developed to suit Alfa Laval, Fristam, Pharma Lab, KSB, Grundfos, many mores.

REFURBISHMENT OF MECHANICAL SEALS

FLUID SEALS INDIA® have Refurbished, Re-Engineered & Indigenized the following models of multinational brands like JOHNCRANE, AESSEAL, SEALOL, SAFEMATIC, EAGLE BURGMANN, FLOWSERVE, LEAK-PROOF, CHESTERTON etc.

The company is being proficiently managed by the Managing Director, Mr. Majid Loriya. Owing to advanced manufacturing unit spread in an area of 2,240 square feet, we manufacture these seals as per the exact specifications and requirements of our clients, in an efficient manner.
